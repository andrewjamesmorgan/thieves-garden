Maidenhead serviced apartment

Welcome to Thieves Garden – a serviced apartment in Maidenhead, Berkshire

Thieves Garden provides well equipped accommodation for anyone looking rooms to rent in Maidenhead. Ideal for those visiting the region who are looking for the comfort of a hotel but with the extra space and independence of a self-catering apartment - without the need for long term contracts or utility bills.

You will be staying in a detached building with a private entrance, but with the advantage of the proprietors living right next door.

We offer flexible letting terms so we're ideal if you're looking to buy a new home and need somewhere to stay that doesn't insist on long notice periods.

Inside the apartment

Sleeps 2-4 people

The ground floor is a large, open-plan room. There's a double bed, and a sofa bed.

Upstairs is a kitchen, bathroom, and a dining/study area.

Bedroom

Double bed. Sheets changed weekly.

Sofa bed that can sleep an extra 2 people.

Bathroom

The bathroom contains a shower, toilet and sink.

Clean towels are provided weekly.

Lounge

The lounge has a sofa bed and TV - complete with satelite channels and on-demand streaming.

Dining/study area

The dining room/study is at the top of the stairs.

There's a dining/work table and four chairs.

Kitchen

The kitchen includes an oven, hob, microwave, fridge-freezer, kettle, and sink.

Cooking utensils, crockery, and cutlery are all provided.

Laundry

This is the one room which you'll share with the our house. The laundry is at the back of the garage, and you enter it through thise side door.

There's a washing machine, drier and a clothes airer.

In the summer, there's a washing line in the garden which you're welcome to use.

Outside the apartment

Outdoor dining on your terrace, shared laundry, and plenty of on-street parking.

Parking

There is plenty of un-restricted, free on-street parking (no yellow lines).

Areas with a yellow line should not be parked on from 7:00 to 9:00 on weekday mornings.

Outdoor dining

The apartment comes with a terrace and picnic table.

Facilities & Services

Everything you need for a comfortable stay

Our aim is to provide everything you need during your stay, all included in the headline price.

That includes WiFi, streaming TV, somewhere to cook, linen and cleaning, parking, ...

TV

In the lounge/bedroom, there's a television.

You have access to several streaming services such as Netflix.

Cleaning

The apartment is cleaned between each tenant, and on each Tuesday.

Clean bedding and towels are provided every week.

Central heating

The apartment is heated by a dedicated gas boiler.

You have control over the temperature from your own smart thermostat. Let us know if you'd like use to set up a heating schedule.

Cooking

Inside the kitchen you have everything that you'd look for – induction hob, oven, fridge-freezer, microwave, pots and pans, toaster, kettle, .... If you find anything missing then just let us know and we'll try to provide it.

Of course if the weather's good (which it normally is!) then you'll want to spend as much time as possible outside, and so you have access to a gas barbecue.

Internet

The apartment has fast internet - wired and wireless (WiFi).

Location

Where to find Thieves Garden

Thieves Garden is located in a quiet area, on the western side of Maidenhead.

Maidenhead Station and town center

It's about 15 minutes' walk from the train station and the town center.

Maidenhead station is on the Elizabeth Line, with frequent trains into London, and to Reading.

Pond House pub

The Pond House is just down the road (turn left at the traffic lights).

It's a friendly local pub with a good selection of beers and a menu of pub classics.

Windsor Castle pub

The Windsor Castle is just down the road (turn right at the traffic lights).

This is a good option if your looking for more of a gastro-pub.

Supermarket

There's a Tesco Metro just a couple of minutes' walk away.

It's open 7am to 10pm Monday through Saturday, and from 8am to 10pm on Sundays.
